Pilk

Pepsi and Milk may seem like an undesirable combination to some, but to me it is the most delectable treat one could ask for. Pilk is an amazing desert option especially if you do not have ice cream to make an Ice Cream Float.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup of skim milk
  • 1/2 cup of flat Pepsi

Instructions

  1. Pour a half cup of skim milk into a cup
  2. Pour a half cup of Pepsi (preferably flat) into the same cup.
  3. Enjoy :)
