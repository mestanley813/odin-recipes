Mounterey Soufflé Salad

This was a dish passed down to me from my Grandma, combining two of her favorite things: tuna and jello. This dish goes excellently with a slice of American single cheese melted on top.

Ingredients

  • 1 package of lemon-flavored gelatin
  • 1 cup hot water and 1/2 cup cold water
  • 2 tbsps lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup Hellman's or Best Foods Real Mayonnaise
  • 1-1/2 cans Star-Kist Tuna
  • 3/4 cup chopped cucumber or celery
  • 1/4 cup sliced stuffed olives
  • 2 tbsps chopped pimento
  • 1/2 tsp grated onion

Instructions

  1. Dissolve gelatin in hot water. Add cold water, lemon juice, real mayonnaise and 1/4 tsp salt. Blend well with rotary beater.
  2. Pour into refrigerator freezing tray. Quick chill in freezing unit (without changning control) 15 to 20 minutes, or until firm about 1 inch from edge but soft in center.
  3. Turn mixture into bowl and whip with rotary beater until fluffy. Fold in remaining ingridients.
  4. Pour in 1-quart mold or individual molds. Chill until firm in refrigerator (not freezing unit) 30 to 60 minutes. Unmold and garnish with salad greens and serve with additional real mayonnaise, if desired.
